5 Things To Do Before a Friend Stops By

Posted by: Rebecca Atwood

I always envision I will host more dinner parties and gatherings at home, but the truth is life often gets busy and those perfect plans don't happen. More often than not I see my friends at impromptu times when one of us texts the other and swings by for a quick visit after work. I don't know about you but my home isn't usually picture perfect for those times! Good friends don't mind of course, but here are my top 5 things I try and do before someone pops by.

1) Refill your water filter. Offering your friend a fresh glass of water when they arrive is always appreciated.
